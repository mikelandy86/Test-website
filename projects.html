HAGS Design 3D

I was assigned to developed an app for HAGS, one of Sweden's largest manufacturers of outdoor furniture. The client wants an app that allows them to visualize their products in augmented reality and create shopping lists. The app supports 9 languages and also provides a configurator for customizing the articles numerous product variants.

The app is available to download on Google Play and the App Store. The app's name is "HAGS Design 3D"
